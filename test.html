About

FernFix was born from a fascination with the intricate architecture of nature. What began as a personal collection of miniature landscapes has evolved into a studio dedicated to creating living art pieces.

Our approach combines traditional horticulture with contemporary design. Each terrarium is carefully composed, considering not just the plants, but the interplay of light, shadow, and form.

We work exclusively with locally sourced materials and propagate our own plants, ensuring each piece is sustainable and uniquely suited to its environment.

The studio collaborates with interior designers, architects, and private collectors to create custom installations that transform spaces.

— FernFix Atelier
